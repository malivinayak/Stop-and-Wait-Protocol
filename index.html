Stop & Wait Protocol

  1. Sender side
    • Rule 1: Sender sends one data packet at a time.
    • Rule 2: Sender sends the next packet only when it receives the acknowledgment of the previous packet or after Time-out-Timer.
  2. Receiver side
    • Rule 1: Receive and then consume the data packet.
    • Rule 2: When the data packet is consumed, receiver sends the acknowledgment to the sender.
